00 / ABOUT THE PROJECT

A tool that saves time, inspires progress and keeps runners running.

Race Planner is designed to revolutionise how runners plan and participate in events. Inspired by the challenges athletes face, the project bridges the gap between training and race day — providing a seamless platform for discovering events, simplifying registration, and enhancing motivation with personalised achievements.

Through detailed research and collaboration with runners and race organisers, the goal was to create a tool that saves time, inspires progress, and fosters a vibrant community — ensuring every runner reaches their goals with confidence.

01 / THE PROBLEM

Runners are everywhere.
The races are scattered.

01Difficulty finding events that suit a runner’s goals and preferences.
02Tedious, clunky registration processes with no saved details.
03Lack of personalised motivation and tools to track progress.
04Challenges in managing travel and race-day logistics.

03 / RESEARCH · COMPETITOR ANALYSIS

I benchmarked the apps runners already use.

Before designing, I analysed leading event and activity apps across user experience, content, functionality and reviews — to find the gap none of them filled for runners.

Spocort

Athletes, coaches & fitness enthusiasts improving training and connecting with peers.

STRENGTH

Clear icons and imagery aid navigation.

GAP

Interface feels overloaded for new users; event search and filtering need work.

Strava

Runners, cyclists & swimmers tracking workouts and competing with friends.

STRENGTH

Intuitive, map-based design with detailed analytics and clean visuals.

GAP

Focused on individual activities, not events — limited event information.

AreaLive

Athletes & fans looking for live streams and sporting events.

STRENGTH

Effective event search by location; modern, appealing visuals.

GAP

Built for viewing, not participating; event info hard to navigate.

Eventbrite

General public booking events of all kinds, including sports.

STRENGTH

Intuitive filtering by category, date and location; up-to-date content.

GAP

Running events aren’t a dedicated category; sport filters lack precision.

↳

The gap: none combine race discovery, in-app registration and runner motivation in one focused place. That became Race Planner’s wedge.
